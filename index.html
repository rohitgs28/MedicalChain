What is the problem we are solving ?
1. People do not have access to their health data history and getting access to the health log involves a complicated and time-consuming process of communicating with insurance companies, hospitals and third party health care providers. eHealth Ontario takes a time period of 30 days for processing applications.
2. The total cost of insurance fraud is estimated to be more than $40 billion per year. Our project aims at using a digital health passport to accurately convey the patient's medical condition to the physician and insurance company thereby ensuring transparency.
3.. People are not aware of new cutting edge technologies such as Medicalchain through which they can pay for their health with cryptocurrencies.
4. With blockchain, big data silos are eliminated and health-care operational processes are automated with smart contracts. Thereby all healthcare facilities will have access to real-time databases which are decentralized and the data is secured with cryptography.
